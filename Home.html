Senior Project Seminar Courses

Tech 4513 - Senior Project Seminar in Tech: "A system implementation course that intergrates theory and practice in design and development of an information technology (IT) - based solution. The students will choose a project, and then research, design, implement, test, document, demonstrate and present results to the class."
